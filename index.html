About Me

The Now

I am currently enrolled at a 14-week programming bootcamp called We Can Code IT in Columbus, Ohio. Along with a moderate sized cohort, I've been tackling "real-world" projects in an agile environment using paired and mob programming.

Background

Working service-industry jobs have been a large part of my adult life. As a bartender for the last 8 years, I have benefited from meeting many, many kinds of interesting people. Creating my own product for the guests and entertaining any creative way I can is something that I hold extreme value to. It wasn't until I started taking on more responsibilities that I considered an exact path of a career change. Specificially, when I adopted the task of counting the bar inventory and doing orders and managing a database. I wanted to know more.

This lead to independent, online coding classes and doing as much research as I had time for to get into this field.
